Ingredients:
 4 fingers plantain
 2 tubers cassava

Directions:
 1. Peel both plantain and cassava
 2. Boil both together for an hour or until they look pale
 3. Pour water off and then get your mortar and pestle
 4. Pound the plantain until gooey
 5. Pound cassava until gooey
 6. Now put both of them back in the mortar and pound till all blended in
 7. Bon Apetit with some soup
